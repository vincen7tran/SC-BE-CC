Hello, Welcome to SmartCar API!

Function Endpoint Description
GET Vehicle Information /vehicles/:id Get the general information regarding a vehicle by its ID.
GET Security Information /vehicles/:id/doors Get information on which doors are locked on a vehicle by its ID.
GET Energy Information (Fuel) /vehicles/:id/fuel Get information on a vehicle's fuel range by its ID.
GET Energy Information (Battery) /vehicles/:id/battery Get information on an electric vehicle's battery range by its ID.
POST Remotely Control Vehicle Engine /vehicles/:id/engine Start or Stop a vehicle's engine by its ID using the "START" or "STOP" commands.
